About

awesome-wipeout is a community-maintained, normalised library of WipEout-universe art and design — and a growing hub of related references and links.

As a WipEout fan and Designer's Republic tragic for many years, I've long admired the collision of art and gameplay that WipEout has always been.

More recently, having a bit of time on my hands between jobs, I started building Lego versions of WipEout ships (I've now done a bunch — AG-Systems, Feisar, Van-Über, Tigron and more coming. Shameless plug: you can grab the build instructions on Gumroad, or kits & instructions from BuildAMOC!). While doing this I needed reference shots and game art from WipEout for the ship designs and then accurate sticker livery.

So down the WipEout rabbit hole I went.

After spending more time than any reasonable middle-aged man should on Reddit and deep into page 10+ of Google results, I was able to collate quite a collection of game references, art, recreations, fonts, vectors and more. All this is out there, but it's not necessarily easy to find.

So at the risk of being sued into oblivion by Sony (please don't!) with the help of my friend Claude Code, I've created awesome-wipeout, a public non-commercial collection of logos, emblems, marks, fonts, graphics, screenshots and links from across the WipEout anti-gravity racing universe. It's not intended to be Google, but it should provide a canonical source and/or links for all the major design & art WipEout references from across the web in 2025/26 for anyone still into this aging franchise to enjoy :) You now know everything I do about WipEout!

I've attempted to respect everyone's published licenses, if you have concerns, check out the Licensing page. If you plan to use any of these assets, please ensure you stay within the artist or provider's published intention. If you work at Sony, look the other way won't you? If you have something to add or contribute, you can open an issue in the GitHub repo and if you're able, just raise a PR.

And importantly, let it be known this is a non-commercial, fan-made archive not officially associated with Sony or anyone involved in the WipEout game series. All original creators retain credit for their work; each asset links to its contributor and licence.
